NLCI Gujarati - Version History

24 May 2024 (Dev NLCI) NLCI Gujarati fonts version 0.301

  • Bold a few glyphs in the Bold weight that were overlooked

26 Apr 2024 (Dev NLCI) NLCI Gujarati fonts version 0.300

  • Use contextual forms with umatra, uumata, and anusvara
  • Improve punctuation

10 Apr 2024 (Dev NLCI) NLCI Gujarati fonts version 0.200

  • Add many Latin and Devanagari characters
  • Latin glyphs are from Charis SIL (https://software.sil.org/charis/) scaled 71%
  • Fixed many conjuncts

29 Aug 2018 (Dev NLCI) NLCI Gujarati fonts version 0.101

  • Fixed a small encoding mixup

24 Sep 2015 (Dev NLCI) NLCI Gujarati fonts version 0.100

  • Latin glyphs are from Charis SIL (https://software.sil.org/charis/)
